Pizza Ovens: Perfect for Authentic Wood-Fired Cooking

Pizza ovens have become increasingly popular among homeowners seeking that authentic, restaurant-quality experience. These specialized cookers use intense, concentrated heat—typically reaching 800-900°F—to cook pizzas in just 60-90 seconds, creating crispy crusts and perfectly melted cheese. Beyond pizzas, modern pizza ovens can handle roasted vegetables, bread, and even meats, making them surprisingly versatile.

Big Horn Outdoors offers premium pizza oven models that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design. Grills: The Versatile Backyard Staple

Grills remain the most popular choice for outdoor cooking, and for good reason. Available in charcoal, gas, or pellet varieties, grills excel at high-heat cooking methods like searing, grilling, and smoking. Gas grills offer convenience and temperature control, charcoal grills provide superior flavor, and pellet grills combine ease with versatility. Most grills feature multiple cooking zones, allowing you to manage different heat levels simultaneously.

Big Horn Outdoors' grill selection includes models for every preference and budget. Their grills are engineered for durability and consistent heat distribution, ensuring professional-grade results whether you're grilling steaks, vegetables, or delicate fish. With features like advanced temperature controls and spacious cooking surfaces, Big Horn Outdoors grills are designed to handle everything your backyard entertaining demands.

Griddles: The Breakfast Champion and Flat-Top Expert

Griddles offer a flat cooking surface perfect for foods that would fall through grill grates. From pancakes and eggs to stir-fries and fajitas, griddles provide even heat distribution across the entire cooking area. They're also excellent for cooking smaller items like shrimp or diced vegetables, and they minimize flare-ups since food sits directly on the surface. Griddles typically range from 300-600 square inches of cooking space.

Big Horn Outdoors recognizes the growing demand for quality griddles and offers both standalone models and grill-griddle combination units. Their griddles feature seasoned steel surfaces and powerful burners that heat evenly, making them ideal for feeding crowds or batch cooking. The versatility of a Big Horn Outdoors griddle means you can expand your outdoor cooking repertoire beyond traditional grilling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you use a pizza oven for cooking things other than pizza?

Absolutely! Pizza ovens excel at roasting meats, baking bread, cooking vegetables, and even preparing desserts. The intense, concentrated heat makes them perfect for any recipe requiring high temperatures. Big Horn Outdoors pizza ovens are designed to handle various cooking methods beyond pizzas.

What's the difference between a gas grill and a charcoal grill?

Gas grills offer convenience, precise temperature control, and quick heating, while charcoal grills provide superior flavor and higher heat capacity. Gas is ideal for weeknight dinners, while charcoal excels at smoking and achieving authentic grill marks. Big Horn Outdoors offers both options to match your preferences.

Is a griddle worth buying if I already have a grill?

Yes! Griddles complement grills perfectly by handling foods that fall through grates and enabling batch cooking. They're essential for breakfast items, stir-fries, and smaller foods. Many Big Horn Outdoors customers choose combination grill-griddle units for maximum outdoor cooking versatility.

How much space do I need for outdoor cooking equipment?

Space requirements vary: pizza ovens need 4x4 feet minimum, grills require 3x3 feet, and griddles need about 3x2 feet. Consider placement, clearance from structures, and traffic flow. Big Horn Outdoors offers various sizes to fit different yard dimensions.

Which outdoor cooker requires the most maintenance?

Pizza ovens need regular cleaning and chimney maintenance, while grills require grate cleaning and gas line checks. Griddles need seasoning and surface care. Big Horn Outdoors equipment is built for durability and includes care guides to simplify maintenance.
