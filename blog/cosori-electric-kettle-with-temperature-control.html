Frequently Asked Questions

What temperature settings does the Cosori Electric Kettle offer?

The Cosori Electric Kettle features six preset temperature options: 160°F for delicate white teas, 175°F for green teas, 195°F for oolong teas, 208°F for black teas, 212°F for boiling water, and a keep-warm function that maintains your selected temperature for up to 30 minutes.

How long does it take to heat water?

The Cosori Electric Kettle uses advanced heating technology to boil water in approximately 5-7 minutes, depending on the amount of water and your selected temperature setting. This makes it faster than stovetop kettles and comparable to other premium electric kettles.

Is the Cosori Electric Kettle safe to use?

Yes, the kettle includes multiple safety features including automatic shut-off when water reaches the desired temperature, boil-dry protection, and cool-touch handles to prevent burns. The stainless steel construction is food-safe and durable.

Can I use the Cosori Electric Kettle for coffee as well as tea?

Absolutely. While the preset temperatures are optimized for different teas, you can use the 212°F setting for coffee brewing or adjust to lower temperatures if you prefer cooler water for pour-over methods.
