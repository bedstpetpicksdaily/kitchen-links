What to Look For

Not all smart bottles cool equally. Look for models that maintain temperatures between 32°F-105°F with ±2°F accuracy. The best options let you set exact temperatures via app—if a bottle only offers "cold" or "hot" presets, you're missing the real value proposition. The infuser basket should be removable, dishwasher-safe, and made from food-grade plastic or stainless steel. Look for models with fine mesh that won't let fruit pulp or loose tea into your water.
