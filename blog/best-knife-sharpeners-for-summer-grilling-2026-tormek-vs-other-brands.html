Why Sharp Knives Matter for Summer Grilling

A sharp knife is a safe knife. Dull blades require more pressure, increasing the risk of slipping and causing injuries. During summer entertaining, when you're juggling multiple tasks and guests are arriving, the last thing you need is a kitchen accident. Sharp knives also provide cleaner cuts on meats and vegetables, reducing oxidation and preserving flavors. Studies show that properly sharpened blades enhance food presentation and cooking results, making them essential for any serious griller.
