Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Tormek professional and home models?

Professional models offer higher horsepower, extended run times, and advanced variable speed controls for heavy-duty commercial use. Home models are more compact, feature preset angles for kitchen knives, and are designed for occasional to regular home use.

Why does Tormek use water-cooled grinding wheels?

Water cooling prevents blade overheating (keeping temperatures below 60°C), which preserves the hardness of high-quality steel, prevents discoloration, and ensures the edge remains sharp longer without metallurgical damage.

Can Tormek sharpeners handle specialty woodworking tools?

Yes. Tormek's comprehensive jig systems and accessory packages enable sharpening of chisels, plane blades, gouges, and other woodworking tools with precision angles tailored to each tool type.

How long do Tormek knife sharpeners typically last?

With proper maintenance, Tormek systems are built to last 20+ years. The water-cooled grinding wheels wear gradually and are easily replaceable, making long-term ownership economical.

Is there a learning curve with Tormek sharpening systems?

Yes, but it's manageable. Tormek provides detailed instruction manuals, video tutorials, and customer support. Most users develop competency within their first 5-10 sharpening sessions.
