Frequently Asked Questions

Can you make ice cream in a regular Instant Pot without an attachment?

No, you cannot make ice cream directly in a standard Instant Pot because it doesn't have freezing capabilities. You need to purchase the official Instant Pot Ice Cream Maker attachment, which is a separate freezer bowl accessory designed specifically for churning.

How long does it take to make ice cream in an Instant Pot Ice Cream Maker?

The churning process typically takes 20-30 minutes to reach soft-serve consistency, depending on your recipe and desired firmness. If you want harder ice cream, you'll need to transfer it to a freezer for an additional 2-4 hours after churning.

Do I need to pre-freeze the Instant Pot Ice Cream bowl before using it?

Yes, the freezer bowl must be frozen for at least 8-24 hours before using it to churn ice cream. Without proper pre-freezing, the attachment won't be cold enough to freeze and churn your mixture.

What size batches can you make with the Instant Pot Ice Cream Maker?

The Instant Pot Ice Cream Maker typically produces about 1.5-2 quarts (1.4-1.9 liters) of ice cream per batch, making it ideal for family servings or small gatherings. Most recipes are designed for this capacity, so you'll need to scale recipes up or down accordingly.
