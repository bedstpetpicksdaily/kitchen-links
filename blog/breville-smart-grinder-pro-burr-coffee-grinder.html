Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Breville Smart Grinder Pro suitable for espresso?

Yes, with 60 grind size settings, it can produce fine grounds suitable for espresso machines, though dedicated espresso grinders may offer more precision at ultra-fine settings.

How long does it take to grind coffee beans?

Grind time varies by setting, typically ranging from 5 to 25 seconds depending on grind size and bean quantity, with the digital timer providing precise control.

Can you use the Breville Smart Grinder Pro for spices?

While technically possible, it's not recommended as oils and residues from spices can affect coffee flavor and damage burrs. Use a dedicated spice grinder instead.
