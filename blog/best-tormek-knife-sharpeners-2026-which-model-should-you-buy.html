The T-4 is a compact, entry-level model ideal for home cooks who sharpen occasionally. The T-8 features a larger grinding wheel and enhanced precision, making it better suited for frequent sharpening or professional use. Both use the same water-cooling technology but the T-8 offers faster grinding and superior consistency.

Can Tormek knife sharpeners handle different blade angles?

Yes. Tormek's precision angle guides accommodate various sharpening angles—typically 15-20 degrees depending on your knife type. This versatility means you can sharpen Japanese knives, Western kitchen knives, and specialized blades with the same system.

How often do I need to replace Tormek grinding stones?

With proper use and maintenance, Tormek's high-quality stones last years before needing replacement. The water-cooling system actually extends stone life by preventing excessive wear and glazing. Most home users can expect 3-5+ years of regular sharpening before considering a stone replacement.

What's included with a Tormek knife sharpener purchase?
