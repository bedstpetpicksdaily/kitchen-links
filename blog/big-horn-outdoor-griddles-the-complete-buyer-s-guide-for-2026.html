What size Big Horn griddle should I choose for my backyard?

Are Big Horn griddles suitable for permanent outdoor installation?

Yes, Big Horn Outdoors offers griddles designed for permanent installation. These models feature reinforced frames and weather-resistant finishes. You can integrate them into built-in outdoor kitchens or place them on permanent bases for reliable long-term use.

Can I use a Big Horn griddle with natural gas or propane?

Most Big Horn griddle models are compatible with both natural gas and propane. Check your specific model's specifications to confirm fuel compatibility. This versatility allows you to choose the fuel source that works best for your setup.
