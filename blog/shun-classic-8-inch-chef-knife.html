Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Shun Classic knife worth the high price?

Yes, for dedicated home cooks and professionals. The Damascus steel blade, exceptional sharpness, and durability provide long-term value that justifies the investment compared to cheaper alternatives that require frequent replacement.

Can I put the Shun Classic knife in the dishwasher?

No, hand washing is required. Dishwasher use can damage the Damascus pattern, dull the blade, and potentially cause corrosion. Always wash by hand and dry immediately.

How often does the Shun Classic knife need sharpening?

With proper care, the Shun Classic maintains its edge for months of regular use. However, it should be honed weekly with a honing steel and professionally sharpened annually or when needed.

What makes Japanese Damascus steel better than other blade materials?

Japanese Damascus steel layers multiple steel types to combine hardness with flexibility. This results in superior edge retention, easier sharpening, and stunning visual appeal compared to single-steel blades.
