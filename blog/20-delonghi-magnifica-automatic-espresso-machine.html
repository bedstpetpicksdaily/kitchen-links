How often should you descale the DeLonghi Magnifica?

Descale every 400-500 cups or every 3-4 months, depending on water hardness. Regular descaling prevents mineral buildup and maintains optimal machine performance.

Can you use pre-ground coffee in the DeLonghi Magnifica?

Yes, there's a bypass doser slot that allows you to use pre-ground coffee if you prefer, though the built-in grinder offers superior freshness and convenience.

What's the water reservoir capacity?
