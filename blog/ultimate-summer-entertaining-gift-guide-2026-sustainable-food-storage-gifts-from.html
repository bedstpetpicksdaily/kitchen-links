How long do Bee's Wrap beeswax wraps last?

With proper care—hand washing in cool water and air drying—Bee's Wrap products typically last up to one year of regular use. Their longevity depends on frequency of use and care taken during washing and storage.

Are Bee's Wrap products completely plastic-free?

Yes, Bee's Wrap is made from organic cotton, beeswax, jojoba oil, and tree resin. At the end of their life, they're completely compostable, making them an environmentally responsible alternative to plastic wrap.

Can Bee's Wrap be used for hot foods?

Bee's Wrap is designed for foods at room temperature or cooler. For hot foods, it's best to let them cool slightly first, as high heat can affect the beeswax coating. They work perfectly for fresh produce, leftovers, and prepared dishes.
