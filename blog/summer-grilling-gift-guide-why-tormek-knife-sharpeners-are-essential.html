The Importance of Sharp Knives for Summer Grilling

Professional chefs understand this fundamental truth: a sharp knife is a safe knife.

Summer entertaining often involves batch food preparation—chopping dozens of vegetables, slicing multiple cuts of meat, and executing precise knife work under time pressure.
