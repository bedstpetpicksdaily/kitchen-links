Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Breville Barista Express come with a warranty?

Yes, Breville includes a 1-year limited warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship, with options to extend coverage.

Can you use pre-ground coffee in the Breville Barista Express?

Yes, there's a bypass doser that allows you to use pre-ground coffee, though the built-in grinder delivers fresher results.

How difficult is the Breville Barista Express to clean?

The machine features a removable group head and basket for easy cleaning. Daily maintenance takes about 5 minutes with regular backflushing recommended.

What's the water temperature range for the Breville Barista Express?

The thermocoil system maintains water temperature between 200-210°F, ideal for optimal espresso extraction.
