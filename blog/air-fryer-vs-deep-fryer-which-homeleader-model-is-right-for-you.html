Understanding Air Fryers vs Deep Fryers

Air fryers use rapid air circulation technology to cook food at high temperatures, creating a crispy exterior with minimal to no oil. Deep fryers, on the other hand, submerge food completely in hot oil, producing that traditional golden-brown finish. HOMELEADER's air fryer models harness advanced convection technology to deliver restaurant-quality results with up to 85% less oil than traditional frying methods, making them ideal for health-conscious home cooks.
