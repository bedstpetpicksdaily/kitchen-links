What's the difference between KitchenAid Artisan and Professional mixers?

The Artisan is designed for home use with a 5-quart bowl, while the Professional model features a larger 6-quart capacity and slightly more powerful motor, making it better suited for commercial or high-volume baking needs.

Can the KitchenAid Artisan handle bread dough?

Yes, the Artisan's powerful motor is specifically designed to knead bread dough efficiently, handling multiple loaves worth of dough without strain.

Are KitchenAid stand mixers worth the price?

For frequent bakers and serious cooks, yes. The Artisan's durability, performance, and extensive attachment options justify the investment through years of reliable use.

What attachments come with the KitchenAid Artisan?

The standard package includes a coated flat mixing paddle, spiral dough hook, and wire whip attachment. Additional attachments like pasta makers and food grinders can be purchased separately.
