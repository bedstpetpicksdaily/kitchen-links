What's the difference between HOMELEADER cold-press juicers and centrifugal juicers?

HOMELEADER cold-press juicers use slow masticating technology that generates minimal heat, preserving more nutrients and enzymes. Centrifugal juicers spin quickly and generate heat, which can degrade delicate nutrients. For fitness goals, cold-press technology offers superior nutritional benefits.

How long does HOMELEADER cold brew coffee stay fresh?

HOMELEADER cold brew concentrate can be refrigerated for up to two weeks. This makes it perfect for meal prep—prepare a batch on Sunday and enjoy fresh cold brew throughout your week of summer workouts.

Most HOMELEADER juicer models can process frozen fruit, though some perform better with room-temperature produce. Check your specific model's specifications, and consider thawing frozen fruit slightly before juicing for optimal extraction and motor longevity.
