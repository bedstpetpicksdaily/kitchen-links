Tormek systems eliminate these compromises by combining automated consistency with precise angle control, water cooling that protects blade integrity, and versatile accessory systems that handle everything from kitchen knives to chisels and plane irons. Yes, Tormek's modular accessory system is specifically designed for versatility. Using different jigs and holders, you can sharpen kitchen knives, chef's knives, woodworking chisels, plane irons, carving tools, and other specialized blades with precision.
