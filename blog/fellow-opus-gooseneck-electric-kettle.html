How long does the Fellow Opus take to heat water?

The Fellow Opus heats water quickly due to its 1500W power element. Most users report reaching boiling temperature in 4-6 minutes, though exact time depends on starting water temperature and desired heat level.

Is the temperature control accurate for different teas and coffee?

Yes, the digital temperature display is highly accurate, allowing you to select optimal temperatures for various beverages. Green tea typically brews at 70-80°C, black tea at 95°C, and coffee at 90-96°C.

What's the water capacity of the Opus kettle?
