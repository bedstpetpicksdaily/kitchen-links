Frequently Asked Questions

What size bread knife is best for summer grilling?

An 8-10 inch bread knife is ideal for most grilling applications, providing enough length to slice through large artisan loaves and grilled breads in a single stroke. Butterfork offers both 8-inch and 10-inch options to suit different preferences and hand sizes.

Can I use a regular chef's knife instead of a bread knife?

While a chef's knife can cut bread, the straight blade tends to compress and tear delicate crumbs rather than cleanly slicing through them. A serrated bread knife like those from Butterfork is specifically designed to grip and cut crusty exteriors while preserving the tender interior.

How do I maintain my Butterfork bread knife?

Always hand wash your Butterfork bread knife with warm soapy water and dry immediately. Store it in a knife stand or sheath to protect the serrated edge. Avoid dishwashers and cutting boards that are too hard, as these can dull the blade over time.

Are Butterfork bread knives suitable for outdoor use?

Yes, Butterfork's stainless steel bread knives are specifically designed to resist corrosion from outdoor humidity and moisture, making them perfect for summer entertaining and grilling season.

What other knives should I pair with a bread knife?

A quality chef's knife for general prep work, a paring knife for detailed tasks, and consider a nakiri knife for vegetables. Butterfork offers complete knife sets that include all essential blades for comprehensive kitchen and grilling needs.
