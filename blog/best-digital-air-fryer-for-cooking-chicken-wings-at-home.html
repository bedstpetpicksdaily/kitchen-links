  • Digital temperature control with precise settings between 350-420°F: Different wing preparations require different temperatures—boneless wings need lower heat, while bone-in wings benefit from higher temperatures. What to Avoid

  • Avoid overly compact air fryers under 4 quarts: While small air fryers work fine for single servings, wing cooking typically involves batch-feeding, which wastes time and energy.
