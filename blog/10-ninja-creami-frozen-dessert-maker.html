Frequently Asked Questions

What types of frozen desserts can the Ninja Creami make?

The Creami creates slushi, sorbet, gelato, smoothie bowls, and Italian ice textures using its five specialized mixing blades designed for different consistencies.

Do I need to pre-freeze ingredients before using the Creami?

Yes, the Ninja Creami requires fully frozen ingredients. You'll need to freeze your juice, smoothie base, or other liquids in the included Creami containers overnight or for several hours.

How long does it take to make a frozen dessert?

Most frozen desserts are ready in under one minute. The Creami's powerful motor quickly processes frozen ingredients into your desired texture.

Is the Ninja Creami easy to clean?

Yes, most parts are removable and dishwasher safe. The containers and blades come apart easily for thorough cleaning and maintenance.

How does the Ninja Creami compare to blenders?

While blenders are versatile, the Creami's specialized design and multiple blades create specific frozen dessert textures that standard blenders cannot replicate as effectively.
