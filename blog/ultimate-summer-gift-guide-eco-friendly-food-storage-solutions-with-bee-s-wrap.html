Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a Bee's Wrap last?

With proper care, a Bee's Wrap typically lasts about one year of regular use. When it reaches the end of its life, you can compost it completely, as it's made from natural, biodegradable materials.

Can Bee's Wrap be used in the freezer?

Yes, Bee's Wrap works well in the freezer. The beeswax actually becomes stiffer in cold temperatures, providing a secure seal for frozen foods. Just ensure you remove it from the freezer a few minutes before opening for easier handling.

Is Bee's Wrap safe for hot foods?

Bee's Wrap is not recommended for hot foods, as heat can damage the beeswax coating. It's best used for room temperature and cold foods. Allow hot leftovers to cool before wrapping.

What foods work best with Bee's Wrap?

Bee's Wrap is perfect for wrapping sandwiches, covering bowls of leftovers, storing fresh produce, wrapping bread, and covering cheese and baked goods. It's ideal for any food that doesn't require extreme temperatures.

How do I clean Bee's Wrap?

Rinse with cool water and mild dish soap using your hands or a soft cloth. Avoid hot water and harsh scrubbing. Air dry completely on a flat surface before storing.
