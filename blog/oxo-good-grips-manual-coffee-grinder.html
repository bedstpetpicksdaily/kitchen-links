Frequently Asked Questions

What grind sizes can the OXO Good Grips produce?

The OXO produces grind sizes ranging from fine to coarse, making it versatile for French press, pour-over, espresso, and Turkish coffee brewing methods.

How long does it take to grind coffee beans?

Grinding typically takes 5-10 minutes depending on grind size and bean quantity. Finer grinds require more time and effort than coarse settings.

Is the OXO Good Grips manual grinder travel-friendly?

Yes, its compact size and lightweight design make it ideal for travel, camping, and portable use. It requires no electricity and fits easily in bags or backpacks.

How many cups of coffee can it grind at once?

The grinder holds approximately 1 cup of beans, yielding enough grounds for 2-3 cups of brewed coffee per fill.
