Understanding Tormek's Sharpening Technology

Tormek revolutionized the knife sharpening industry with their water-cooled grinding wheel system. This patented technology uses a combination of slow-speed grinding wheels (around 90 RPM) and water cooling to sharpen blades without overheating. The water serves multiple purposes: it cools the blade to prevent tempering damage, lubricates the grinding surface, and carries away metal particles. This approach is fundamentally different from high-speed grinders that can compromise blade hardness through excessive heat.

The Tormek system includes precision guides and jigs that ensure consistent blade angles, eliminating the guesswork from manual sharpening. Their stone conditioning system maintains the grinding wheel's performance over thousands of sharpenings, making these systems incredibly durable investments. For both kitchen knives and specialized woodworking blades, Tormek's engineering delivers professional-grade results in your home workshop.
