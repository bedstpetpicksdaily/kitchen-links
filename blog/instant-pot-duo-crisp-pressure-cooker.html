Frequently Asked Questions

Can the Instant Pot Duo Crisp air fry as well as pressure cook?

Yes, the Duo Crisp has both pressure cooking and air frying capabilities in one unit. The air fryer function uses a crisping lid that heats from above, allowing you to achieve crispy textures on foods like chicken wings, french fries, and vegetables without needing a separate appliance.

What size is the Instant Pot Duo Crisp and how many people does it serve?

The Instant Pot Duo Crisp comes in an 8-quart capacity, which is large enough to cook meals for 8+ people or meal prep for the week. The 8-quart size makes it ideal for families or batch cooking, though it takes up more counter and storage space than smaller models.

Is the Instant Pot Duo Crisp worth the price compared to regular Instant Pots?

The Duo Crisp costs significantly more than a standard Instant Pot because you're getting the air fryer function built-in, eliminating the need to buy a separate air fryer. If you regularly use both pressure cooking and air frying, it can be a worthwhile investment; however, if you only pressure cook, a basic Instant Pot is more budget-friendly.

How long does it take to preheat the Instant Pot Duo Crisp for air frying?

The air fryer function typically preheats in 3-5 minutes, which is faster than most traditional ovens but slightly slower than some standalone air fryers. Preheating time can vary depending on the temperature setting you choose.
