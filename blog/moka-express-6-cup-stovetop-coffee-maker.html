Frequently Asked Questions

How many cups of coffee does the Moka Express 6-cup make?

The 6-cup model produces approximately 9 ounces of strong espresso-style coffee, or about 3-6 servings depending on cup size.

Is the Moka Express safe to use on all stovetops?

Yes, the Moka Express works on gas and electric stovetops. However, avoid induction cooktops unless you purchase a special induction adapter.

How do I clean and maintain my Moka Express?

Rinse all parts with hot water immediately after use and let them air dry. Never use a dishwasher. The rubber gasket should be replaced every 1-2 years.

What's the difference between the Moka Express and electric espresso machines?

The Moka Express is manual and stovetop-powered, making it more affordable and simpler, while electric machines offer better temperature control and consistency.
