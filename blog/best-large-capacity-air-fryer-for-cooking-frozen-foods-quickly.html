  • Even heat distribution and powerful wattage (1700W+): Frozen foods need consistent, intense heat to cook through quickly without drying out edges. Higher wattage models reach optimal temperature faster and maintain it better when you're loading in a full batch of frozen chicken, fries, or vegetables.
  • Presets or manual controls specifically for frozen foods: The best large capacity models include dedicated frozen food settings that automatically adjust time and temperature.
