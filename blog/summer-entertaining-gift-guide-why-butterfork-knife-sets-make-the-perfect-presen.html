What's included in a typical Butterfork knife set?

Butterfork offers various set configurations, typically including a chef knife, paring knife, and bread knife. Many sets come with a knife stand for elegant storage and display. Check individual product descriptions for exact inclusions.

Are Butterfork knives suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. While Butterfork knives are professional-grade, their ergonomic design and balanced weight distribution make them accessible for cooks of all skill levels. They're particularly great for beginners who want to learn proper technique with quality tools.
