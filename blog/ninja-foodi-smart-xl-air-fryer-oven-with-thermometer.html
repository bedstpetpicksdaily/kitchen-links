How much counter space does this actually need?

Plan for roughly 24 inches wide by 16 inches deep, plus 4-5 inches of clearance on all sides for air circulation. If your counter is already cramped, measure before buying—this isn't something you can squeeze into a tight spot.

Does the smart thermometer work as advertised?

Yes, it connects reliably via Bluetooth and gives accurate temperature readings within 1-2 degrees. The app interface is intuitive. However, the probe does require a clear line of sight to the oven, so placement matters—stick it in thick chicken breasts or roasts, not thin cutlets.

Is this better than buying a separate air fryer and keeping my regular oven?

If you have the counter space and budget, this replaces both for most everyday cooking. You'll still use a traditional oven for large batch baking or Thanksgiving turkeys, but for roasted proteins, vegetables, frozen foods, and reheating, this handles it faster and with better results than either appliance alone.
