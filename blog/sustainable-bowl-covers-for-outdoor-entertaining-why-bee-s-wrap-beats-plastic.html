Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Bee's Wrap bowl covers last?

With proper care, Bee's Wrap reusable beeswax wraps last up to one year. To extend their lifespan, hand wash them in cool water with mild soap and air dry. Avoid hot water and direct heat, which can damage the beeswax coating.

Can I use Bee's Wrap for hot foods?

Bee's Wrap is best used for cool to room-temperature foods. While beeswax has a melting point around 62°C (144°F), it's recommended to let hot foods cool before covering them to maintain the wrap's integrity and effectiveness.

Are Bee's Wrap bowl covers safe for all food types?

Yes, Bee's Wrap is safe for all food types including fruits, vegetables, cheeses, bread, and prepared dishes. The natural beeswax won't transfer any harmful chemicals to your food, making it ideal for families concerned about health and sustainability.

How do I clean Bee's Wrap after outdoor entertaining?

Simply rinse Bee's Wrap under cool running water with a mild dish soap and your fingers. Air dry flat or hang it up. For stubborn residue, use a soft cloth. Never use hot water or place in the dishwasher, as this damages the beeswax coating.

What happens to Bee's Wrap when it reaches the end of its life?

Bee's Wrap is fully compostable and biodegradable. When it's no longer usable, simply add it to your compost bin where it will naturally break down within months, returning valuable nutrients to the soil.
