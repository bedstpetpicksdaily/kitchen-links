Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Cuisinart Burr Coffee Maker have adjustable grind settings?

Yes, it features multiple grind size settings, allowing you to customize coarseness from fine to extra coarse grounds based on your brewing preferences.

Can you use pre-ground coffee in this machine?

Absolutely. While the built-in burr grinder is convenient, you can bypass it and add pre-ground coffee directly to the filter basket.

How long does it take to brew a full pot?

Brewing a full 14-cup carafe typically takes 10-12 minutes, depending on grind size and water temperature.

Is the carafe dishwasher safe?

Most Cuisinart Burr Coffee Maker carafes are dishwasher safe, though checking your specific model's manual is recommended for longevity.
