Frequently Asked Questions

What does 7-in-1 mean on the Instant Pot Duo?

The 7-in-1 refers to seven different cooking functions: pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, sauté pan, yogurt maker, and food warmer. This allows you to replace multiple kitchen appliances with one device.

Is the 6-quart Instant Pot Duo worth buying over smaller models?

The 6-quart model is ideal for families of 4+ or meal preppers who need larger batch cooking capacity. If you cook for fewer people, smaller 3-quart or 5-quart models may be more suitable and affordable.

How long does it take to cook meals in the Instant Pot Duo?

Cooking times are significantly faster than traditional methods—typically 1/3 the time of slow cooking. Most meals cook in 15-45 minutes, plus 5-15 minutes for pressure buildup and release.

Is the Instant Pot Duo difficult to use for beginners?

While there's a learning curve, the 14 pre-programmed settings and intuitive controls make it manageable for beginners. We recommend reading the manual and starting with simple recipes before advancing to complex dishes.
