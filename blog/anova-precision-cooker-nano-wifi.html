How long does it take to cook food with the Anova Nano?

Cooking times vary dramatically by what you're cooking, but here's what I've consistently found: chicken breasts cook perfectly in 1.5 to 2 hours at 165°F, salmon fillets need 25-35 minutes at 125°F, and steak (medium-rare) takes about 1.5 hours at 129°F.
