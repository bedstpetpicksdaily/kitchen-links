Bee's Wrap products typically last approximately one year with regular use. The lifespan depends on frequency of use and care. After one year, the wraps are fully compostable and can be added to garden compost or municipal composting programs.

Can you wash and reuse beeswax wraps?

Yes! Bee's Wrap wraps are designed for frequent washing. Simply rinse with cool water and mild soap, then air dry flat. Avoid hot water and dishwashers as heat can damage the beeswax coating. With proper care, wraps remain functional throughout their one-year lifespan.
