Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a chef knife and a nakiri knife?

Chef knives are versatile workhorses with curved blades ideal for rocking and slicing, while nakiri knives feature flat blades perfect for precise vegetable cuts. Butterfork offers both options to complement different cutting styles and entertaining needs.

Do Butterfork knife sets come with storage?

Yes, Butterfork's comprehensive knife sets include premium knife stands designed to organize and protect your blades while keeping them accessible during meal preparation.

How do I maintain my Butterfork chef knives?

Hand wash your Butterfork knives immediately after use and dry them promptly. Use a knife stand for storage rather than a drawer. Hone regularly and professionally sharpen annually to maintain optimal edge retention and performance.

Are Butterfork knives suitable for outdoor entertaining?

Absolutely. Butterfork chef knives and specialized collections are perfect for outdoor entertaining prep. Their durable construction, sharp edges, and ergonomic design handle high-volume food preparation efficiently.

What makes a good bread knife for entertaining?

Quality bread knives feature serrated edges that cut cleanly through crusty exteriors without crushing soft interiors. Butterfork's bread knives are specifically designed for this purpose, making them ideal for serving fresh baked goods at gatherings.
