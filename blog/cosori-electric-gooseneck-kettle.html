Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Cosori kettle keep water hot?

Yes, the Cosori features a keep-warm function that maintains your desired temperature for up to 60 minutes, perfect for multiple servings.

Is the gooseneck spout easy to clean?

The spout design is relatively easy to clean, though regular descaling is recommended to prevent mineral buildup in hard water areas.

What's the water capacity?

The Cosori typically holds 0.8-1.0 liters, making it ideal for 2-4 cups of tea or coffee.

Can you use it for brewing different types of tea?

Absolutely. The preset temperature settings are designed for various beverages including green tea, black tea, white tea, and coffee brewing.
