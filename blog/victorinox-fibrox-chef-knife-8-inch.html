Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Victorinox Fibrox Chef Knife dishwasher safe?

While technically dishwasher safe, hand washing is recommended to preserve the blade's sharpness and extend its lifespan. Avoid abrasive cleaners and dry immediately after washing.

How often should I sharpen the Victorinox Fibrox Chef Knife?

With regular honing using a honing steel every few weeks, you can maintain the knife's edge for months. Professional sharpening is recommended once or twice yearly depending on usage frequency.

Can the Victorinox Fibrox Chef Knife handle frozen foods?

While capable of handling some frozen items, it's best used on room-temperature ingredients. Attempting to cut very hard frozen foods may damage the blade edge.
