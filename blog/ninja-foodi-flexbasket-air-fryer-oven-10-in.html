The trade-off: this flexibility costs $80-$120 more than single-basket models. Real-world value depends on your cooking style. Batch cookers making the same recipe in bulk? Minimal benefit. People cooking multiple components at once? It saves 15-20 minutes per session. Calculate your actual cooking pattern—if you use this feature 2-3 times weekly, it's probably worth it. If you're just doing standard air fryer meals, you're overpaying.
