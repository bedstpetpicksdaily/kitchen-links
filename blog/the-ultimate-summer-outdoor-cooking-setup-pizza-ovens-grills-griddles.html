Big Horn Outdoors offers both options, and your choice depends on cooking style preferences and fuel accessibility. Many enthusiasts prefer wood-fired for authentic flavor but appreciate gas models for reliability and ease of use.

Can I leave Big Horn Outdoors outdoor cooking equipment outside year-round?

Most Big Horn Outdoors equipment features weather-resistant stainless steel and durable materials designed for outdoor exposure.
