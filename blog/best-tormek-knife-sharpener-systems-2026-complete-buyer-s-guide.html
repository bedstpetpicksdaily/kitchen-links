Can Tormek sharpen Japanese knives safely?

Yes, Tormek is excellent for Japanese knives. The water-cooled system prevents heat damage that can compromise the temper of high-carbon Japanese steel. The precise angle control allows you to maintain proper edge geometry on traditional Japanese blade shapes.

Frequency depends on use intensity. Professional kitchen environments might sharpen weekly, while home cooks typically sharpen monthly or quarterly. Tormek's stropping wheels help maintain edges between sharpening sessions, extending intervals between major maintenance.
