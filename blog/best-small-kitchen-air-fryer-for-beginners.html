    Frequently Asked Questions

    What size air fryer is best for a small kitchen?

    For small kitchens, look for air fryers between 2-4 quarts, which take up minimal counter space while still fitting a meal for 2-4 people. Compact models like the Cosori 2.5-quart or Ninja 3.3-quart are popular choices that fit easily in cabinets or corners.

    Do beginner air fryers really cook food faster than an oven?

    Yes, air fryers typically cook 30% faster than traditional ovens because hot air circulates directly around the food. For example, frozen French fries cook in 12-15 minutes in an air fryer versus 25-30 minutes in a conventional oven.

    How much should I spend on a beginner air fryer?

    A quality beginner air fryer ranges from $50-$150, with budget options like the GoWISE USA offering basic features, while mid-range models like Instant Vortex provide better build quality and preset functions. Spending under $100 is perfectly adequate to learn if air frying is right for you.

    Do I need an air fryer with lots of presets or is a basic model enough?

    A basic air fryer with just temperature and timer controls is sufficient for beginners since most foods cook similarly; however, preset buttons for chicken, fries, or vegetables can save time and reduce guesswork. Start with a simple model and upgrade later if you want more features.
