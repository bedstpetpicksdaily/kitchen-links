What to Avoid

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Buying incompatible valve accessories for your specific Instant Pot model—always verify your pot's size and generation before purchasing, as not all accessories work universally across the product line.
  • Neglecting to clean and maintain your pressure release valve regularly, which can lead to steam leaks, reduced functionality, and potential safety hazards over time.
  • Ignoring the instruction manual and using accessories improperly—many people don't realize that natural pressure release and quick release require different techniques, and improper use can compromise cooking results.

Expert Tips for Best Results

  • Always allow your Instant Pot to fully pressurize before adjusting or using any valve accessories, and remember that proper sealing begins with a clean rubber gasket—inspect it monthly and replace annually for optimal performance.
  • Master the difference between quick release (for foods that can handle sudden pressure drops) and natural release (for delicate dishes like ribs or cheesecake) to dramatically improve your cooking outcomes and texture consistency.
  • Keep a kitchen towel nearby when releasing pressure to safely direct steam away from your face and hands, and always position the valve away from cabinets to prevent moisture damage and condensation buildup.
