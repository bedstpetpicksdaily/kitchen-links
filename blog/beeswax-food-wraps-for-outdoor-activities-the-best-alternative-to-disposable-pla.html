Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Bee's Wrap products last?

With proper care and storage, Bee's Wrap products typically last up to one year of regular use. Proper care includes hand washing with cool water and mild soap, air drying flat, and storing in a cool place away from direct sunlight and heat sources.

Can I use beeswax wraps in the microwave or with hot foods?

No, beeswax wraps are not suitable for microwaves or direct contact with hot foods, as heat can damage the beeswax coating. They work best for cold foods and room-temperature items. Allow hot foods to cool before wrapping.

Are Bee's Wrap products vegan-friendly?

Traditional Bee's Wrap contains beeswax, which is not vegan. However, the brand offers plant-based alternatives using plant waxes for customers seeking vegan options. Check their product line for vegan-certified options.

How do I clean and maintain my beeswax wraps?

Hand wash your wraps with cool water and mild soap immediately after use. Air dry them completely on a clean surface or clothesline. Avoid hot water, dishwashers, and dryers, as heat can compromise the beeswax coating.

Can beeswax wraps replace all my food storage needs?

Beeswax wraps work wonderfully for most foods but are best suited for cold and room-temperature items. For hot foods or extended refrigeration, you may still need complementary storage solutions, though wraps handle most outdoor activity food storage needs excellently.
