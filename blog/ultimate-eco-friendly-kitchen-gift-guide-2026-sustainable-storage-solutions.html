With proper care, Bee's Wrap beeswax wraps last up to one year or longer. They can be washed with cool water and mild soap, then air-dried. The natural beeswax coating may need refreshing annually for optimal performance.

Are Bee's Wrap products safe for all foods?

Bee's Wrap works best with most foods, but should not be used with raw meat or fish. The wraps are perfect for cheese, produce, bread, and prepared foods, making them versatile for most kitchen storage needs.

Yes, Bee's Wrap products are fully compostable at the end of their life, making them a truly zero-waste solution. This natural end-of-life option sets them apart from other reusable storage alternatives.
