Frequently Asked Questions

Is Le Creuset Dutch Oven worth the price?

Yes, if you're a frequent cook. Le Creuset's superior enamel coating and construction last 20+ years, making the cost-per-use reasonable over time compared to budget alternatives that may need replacement.

Can you use Le Creuset on all cooktops?

Le Creuset works on gas, electric, induction, and ceramic cooktops. It's also safe for oven use up to 500°F and can be used on open flames and grills.

What size Dutch Oven should I buy?

The 5.5-quart size is most versatile for home cooking, suitable for family meals and batch cooking. Smaller 2-3 quart sizes work for couples, while 7+ quarts suit larger families.

How do you care for a Le Creuset Dutch Oven?

Hand wash with warm soapy water and dry immediately. Avoid sudden temperature changes. The enamel coating doesn't require seasoning like bare cast iron, making maintenance straightforward.
