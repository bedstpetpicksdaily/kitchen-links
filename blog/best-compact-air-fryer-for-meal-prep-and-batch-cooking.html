    What size air fryer is best for meal prep and batch cooking?

    Look for air fryers with at least 4-6 quarts of capacity to fit multiple portions in a single batch, which saves time during meal prep. A compact model between 4-5.5 quarts offers the sweet spot between countertop space and cooking volume, allowing you to prepare meals for 4-5 days without multiple cooking cycles.

    Can you stack food in a compact air fryer for batch cooking?

    Yes, but carefully—most compact air fryers support stacking racks or trays (sold separately or included), which doubles your cooking capacity without taking up extra countertop space. Always check your specific model's manual to ensure it's compatible with accessories and maintains proper air circulation for even cooking.

    How long does it take to meal prep with a compact air fryer?

    A compact air fryer typically cooks meals 30-40% faster than a conventional oven, and with batch cooking, you can prepare 3-5 days of meals in 45 minutes to an hour. The exact time depends on your meal complexity, but most proteins and vegetables cook in 10-20 minutes per batch.

    Most compact air fryers preheat in 2-3 minutes compared to 10-15 minutes for traditional ovens, making them significantly more efficient for meal prep sessions. This faster preheat time means you can start cooking immediately and complete your entire meal prep faster.
