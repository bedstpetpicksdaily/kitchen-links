Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Breville Barista Express come with a milk frother?

Yes, it includes a manual steam wand that allows you to steam and froth milk for cappuccinos and lattes. The learning curve for milk frothing is similar to the espresso extraction process.

How often do I need to clean the Barista Express?

Daily backflushing of the group head is recommended, with weekly deep cleaning of removable parts. Descaling should be done monthly depending on your water hardness to maintain optimal performance.

Can you adjust the grind size on this espresso machine?

Absolutely. The built-in burr grinder features micro-adjustments that allow precise control over grind size, which is crucial for dialing in the perfect espresso shot.

Is the Breville Barista Express suitable for beginners?

Yes, but with caveats. It's beginner-friendly in terms of interface design, but mastering espresso extraction and milk frothing requires practice and patience. Many users find the learning process rewarding.
