Why This Works for This Situation

Basket-style air fryers heat air more efficiently in a confined space, meaning your food cooks faster and more evenly than in larger oven models—this faster feedback loop helps beginners learn what's working and adjust techniques quickly. The exposed basket also makes it easy to shake or flip food mid-cooking without removing a tray, which is less intimidating than working with sliding racks.
