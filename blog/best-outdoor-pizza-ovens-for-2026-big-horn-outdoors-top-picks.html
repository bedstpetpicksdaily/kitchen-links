Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between wood-fired and gas pizza ovens?

Wood-fired ovens provide authentic flavor and impressive heat (up to 900°F), while gas models offer convenient ignition and precise temperature control. Big Horn Outdoors offers both types to match your cooking preferences and lifestyle.

How much space do I need for an outdoor pizza oven?

Compact models require as little as 3x3 feet, while larger units need 6x6 feet or more. Big Horn Outdoors offers various sizes, so measure your patio and consider ventilation space before purchasing.

Are Big Horn Outdoors pizza ovens weatherproof?

Yes, Big Horn Outdoors pizza ovens feature weather-resistant construction with protective covers included. They're engineered to withstand rain, snow, and UV exposure, though covering them during off-season extends their lifespan.

How long does it take to cook a pizza in a Big Horn Outdoors oven?

Wood-fired ovens cook pizzas in 60-90 seconds once properly preheated, while gas models typically take 2-3 minutes. The exact time depends on temperature and pizza thickness.

Do I need professional installation for a Big Horn Outdoors pizza oven?

While some compact models can be assembled at home, larger units benefit from professional installation. Big Horn Outdoors can recommend certified installers in your area for optimal setup.
