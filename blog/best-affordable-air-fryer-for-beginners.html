What to Look For

  • Capacity Between 3-5 Quarts: Beginners don't need massive cooking space yet. A 3.5 to 5-quart basket is perfect for individuals, couples, or small families while keeping the price down and taking up minimal counter space.
  • Simple, Intuitive Controls: Skip the digital touchscreen models with 15 preset functions. Look for straightforward dials or basic buttons for temperature and time—you want to focus on learning to cook, not deciphering a complicated interface.
  • Honest Wattage (1400-1700W): Lower wattage doesn't mean worse results, but you want enough power to crisp food properly. Models in this range deliver consistent heating without being overly complicated or expensive.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size air fryer should I get as a beginner?

For a beginner cooking for 1-2 people, a 2-3 quart air fryer is ideal and takes up minimal counter space. If you're cooking for a family of 4+, opt for a 5.8-6 quart model so you can cook larger portions without crowding. Most affordable beginner models fall into these sizes and offer the best value.

Is a cheap air fryer worth buying or will it break quickly?

Budget air fryers from reputable brands like Cosori, Instant, and Philips are reliable and often come with 1-2 year warranties. The main difference between cheap and expensive models is typically cooking capacity and extra preset functions, not durability—most will last 3-5 years with normal use.

Do I really need an air fryer or is it just a kitchen gadget?

Air fryers are genuinely useful if you cook frozen foods, want crispier results than microwaving, or prefer less oil than traditional frying. However, if you already own a convection oven and rarely cook fried foods, it may be redundant—consider your actual cooking habits before buying.

What features do I actually need in a beginner air fryer?

You only need a temperature dial/buttons, a timer, and a basket—fancy digital displays and 20+ presets are nice but unnecessary. Look for models with dishwasher-safe baskets and at least 1,400 watts of power to ensure consistent, fast cooking results.
