Frequently Asked Questions

How many cups does the Chemex 3-Cup make?

The Chemex 3-Cup brews approximately 3-4 standard coffee cups (about 17-20 ounces) per brewing cycle, making it ideal for individuals or small households.

What type of filters does the Chemex 3-Cup use?

Chemex uses proprietary bonded paper filters that are thicker than standard coffee filters, resulting in cleaner, brighter coffee by removing more oils and sediment.

Is the Chemex 3-Cup dishwasher safe?

Yes, the Chemex glass carafe and collar are dishwasher safe, making cleanup convenient. The wooden collar should be hand-washed to maintain its appearance and longevity.

How long does brewing take with a Chemex?

A typical Chemex brew cycle takes 4-5 minutes from start to finish, depending on your pouring technique and coffee grind size.

Can I use regular coffee filters in a Chemex?

While possible, using genuine Chemex filters is recommended as they're specifically designed for the brewer's unique shape and produce optimal coffee extraction.
