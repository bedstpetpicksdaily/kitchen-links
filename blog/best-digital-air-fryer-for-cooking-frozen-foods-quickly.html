    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can you cook frozen food directly in an air fryer without thawing?

    Yes, air fryers are excellent for cooking frozen foods directly without thawing. Most frozen items like fries, chicken nuggets, and vegetables cook 20-30% faster in an air fryer than a traditional oven, and you'll get crispier results with little to no added oil.

    What temperature and time should I use for frozen foods in an air fryer?

    Most frozen foods cook best at 380-400°F, though some items like frozen vegetables work well at 350°F. As a general rule, reduce your conventional oven time by about 25% and check for doneness a few minutes early, as air fryers cook much faster than traditional ovens.

    How do I know which air fryer model is best for cooking frozen foods quickly?

    Look for air fryers with at least 4-6 quart capacity and wattage between 1400-1700W for the fastest cooking times. Digital models with preset buttons for frozen foods and adjustable temperature controls give you the most flexibility and consistency for various frozen items.

    Do I need to preheat my air fryer before cooking frozen foods?

    Preheating for 3-5 minutes helps achieve the crispiest results and more consistent cooking, though many frozen foods cook well without preheating due to the air fryer's intense heat. Check your air fryer's manual, as some models with rapid heating technology don't require preheating.
