How much faster is the Max compared to standard Instant Pot models?

Is the smart app connectivity actually useful or pure marketing?

It's useful if you're already a smartphone integrator. You can start cooking remotely, check progress without opening the lid, and adjust settings from the couch—handy for timing dinner around erratic schedules. But it's not essential. The Max functions identically without the app. Don't buy this model solely for connectivity; view it as a bonus feature for people who already live in their phone ecosystem.

What's the real difference between the 6-quart Max and the 8-quart model?

The 6-quart sweet spot handles a week's meal prep for a family of four without demanding prime kitchen real estate. For most households, bigger isn't better—it's just bigger.
