What Are Reusable Beeswax Food Wraps?

Unlike plastic wrap, these wraps mold to any shape when warmed by your hands, creating an airtight seal that keeps food fresh. Bee's Wrap pioneered this category and remains the gold standard in the market. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Bee's Wrap last?

With proper care—hand washing in cool water and air drying—Bee's Wrap lasts approximately one year. Most users get 200+ uses per wrap before replacing. Store in a cool place away from direct heat.

Can you use Bee's Wrap in the dishwasher?

No. Bee's Wrap must be hand washed in cool water with mild soap. Hot water and dishwashers will damage the beeswax coating. This actually extends the product's life and maintains its effectiveness.

Are Bee's Wrap wraps safe for hot foods?

Yes. The beeswax coating is food-safe and non-toxic. However, avoid wrapping foods hotter than your hand can comfortably touch, as extreme heat may damage the wrap. They're perfect for warm (not hot) leftovers.

What makes Bee's Wrap better than homemade beeswax wraps?

Bee's Wrap uses premium organic cotton and sustainably sourced beeswax with optimal resin ratios for superior durability and stickiness. Their manufacturing ensures consistent quality that DIY versions struggle to achieve.

Can Bee's Wrap be composted?

Yes. When Bee's Wrap reaches the end of its life, it's 100% compostable. Simply add it to your home compost pile or green waste bin—it will break down completely within months, unlike plastic wrap.
