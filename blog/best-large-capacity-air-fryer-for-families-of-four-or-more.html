    What size air fryer do I need for a family of 4?

    For a family of four, you'll want an air fryer with at least 5.8-6.5 quarts of capacity, though 7+ quarts gives you more flexibility for larger meals and batch cooking. A larger capacity means you can cook enough food in one go without crowding the basket, which ensures even cooking and crispy results.

    Can you cook for 4 people in a regular air fryer?

    Regular air fryers (3-4 quarts) can technically cook for four people, but you'll likely need to cook in batches or use smaller portions, which is time-consuming. Large capacity air fryers (6+ quarts) are designed specifically for family-sized meals and let you prepare complete dinners for four in a single batch.

    Is a large air fryer worth it for families?

    Yes, a large air fryer is worth the investment for families because it saves cooking time by eliminating batch cooking, reduces energy costs compared to using your oven, and gives you more versatility for meal planning.
