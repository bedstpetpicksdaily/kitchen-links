Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Bee's Wrap products actually last?

With proper care and washing, Bee's Wrap products typically last up to one year. The lifespan depends on frequency of use and washing methods. Hand washing in cool water extends durability compared to frequent dishwasher cycles.

Can I use Bee's Wrap with hot foods?

No, Bee's Wrap is designed for room temperature and cold foods only. Beeswax has a low melting point (around 62-64°C), so hot foods can damage the wrap. Allow hot foods to cool completely before wrapping.

Are Bee's Wrap products safe for storing raw meat?

Bee's Wrap is not recommended for raw meat storage due to contamination risks. These products work best with vegetables, cheese, bread, prepared meals, and leftovers. Use separate cutting boards and surfaces for raw meat as always.

How do I clean and maintain Bee's Wrap bowl covers and bread bags?

Wash with cool water and mild soap using your hands or a soft sponge. You can also use the dishwasher's top rack on low temperature. Lay flat to dry. Avoid hot water and high heat, which can damage the beeswax coating.

Is Bee's Wrap suitable for people with bee allergies?

If you have a severe bee allergy, consult your healthcare provider before using Bee's Wrap products, as they contain beeswax. However, beeswax itself rarely causes allergic reactions since it contains minimal bee proteins.
