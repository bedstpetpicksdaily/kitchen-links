Instant Pot Buying Guide: 3 Things to Look For

  • Capacity Size: Instant Pots come in 3, 6, and 8-quart sizes. Choose based on your household size and cooking needs—larger capacities cost more but offer better value for families.
  • Number of Functions: Basic models offer 7-in-1 functionality, while premium versions include air frying, sous vide, and WiFi connectivity. Consider which features you'll actually use to avoid overpaying.
  • Build Quality & Warranty: Look for models with stainless steel construction, quality seals, and at least a 2-year manufacturer warranty to ensure longevity and reliable customer support.

Frequently Asked Questions About Instant Pots

Are Instant Pots safe to use?

Yes, Instant Pots are extremely safe. All models feature multiple safety mechanisms including pressure regulators, safety valves, and thermal overload protection. They've been tested to meet UL, FCC, and CE safety standards.

How long do Instant Pots last?

With proper care, an Instant Pot can last 5-10+ years. The sealing ring and pressure valve may need replacement after heavy use, but these are inexpensive and easy to swap out, making maintenance cost-effective.
