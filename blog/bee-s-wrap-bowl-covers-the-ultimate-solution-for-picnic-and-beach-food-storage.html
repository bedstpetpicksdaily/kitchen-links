With proper care and hand washing, Bee's Wrap covers typically last up to one year. When they reach the end of their life, they're completely compostable and biodegradable.

No, Bee's Wrap should always be hand washed in cool water with mild soap. Hot water and dishwashers can damage the beeswax coating.
