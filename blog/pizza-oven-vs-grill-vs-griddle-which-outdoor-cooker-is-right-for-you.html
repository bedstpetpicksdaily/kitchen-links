Pizza Ovens: The Ultimate Wood-Fired Experience

Pizza ovens have become the centerpiece of outdoor entertaining for good reason. These specialized cookers reach extremely high temperatures—often 700-900°F—allowing you to bake authentic Neapolitan-style pizzas in just 60-90 seconds. Beyond pizza, wood-fired ovens can roast vegetables, bake bread, cook meats, and even desserts, making them incredibly versatile despite their specialty name.

Big Horn Outdoors offers premium pizza ovens that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern convenience. Their models feature superior insulation, efficient wood burning chambers, and durable construction that withstands years of entertaining. Whether you prefer a traditional dome design or a contemporary look, Big Horn Outdoors' pizza oven collection delivers authentic results that impress guests and create unforgettable dining moments.

Grills: The Classic Outdoor Cooking Powerhouse

Grills remain the most popular outdoor cooking appliance for American backyards. Available in gas, charcoal, and pellet varieties, grills excel at high-heat cooking—perfect for steaks, burgers, chicken, and kebabs. They heat quickly, offer excellent temperature control, and are relatively affordable compared to other outdoor cooking methods. Most grills feature multiple cooking zones, allowing you to manage different temperature levels simultaneously.

Big Horn Outdoors' grill collection includes high-performance models equipped with advanced features like multi-burner systems, precision temperature gauges, and expandable cooking surfaces. Their grills are engineered for durability and consistent heat distribution, ensuring professional-quality results every time you cook. Whether you're a weekend griller or serious outdoor chef, Big Horn Outdoors has a grill that meets your performance demands.

Griddles: Flat-Top Cooking Perfection

Griddles feature a large, flat cooking surface that's ideal for cooking multiple items simultaneously—think eggs, pancakes, fajitas, stir-fries, and burgers all at once. The even heat distribution across the griddle surface prevents hot spots and allows for excellent temperature control. Griddles are particularly popular for family-style cooking and commercial-inspired breakfasts.

Big Horn Outdoors' griddles combine spacious cooking surfaces with powerful heating elements to deliver restaurant-quality results in your backyard. From intimate family breakfasts to entertaining large groups, Big Horn Outdoors griddles make flat-top cooking accessible and enjoyable for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a pizza oven be used for cooking other foods besides pizza?

Absolutely! Pizza ovens excel at cooking roasted vegetables, bread, meats, and even desserts. The high heat makes them versatile for any food that benefits from wood-fired flavor and intense heat.

Which cooker heats up the fastest?

Gas grills heat up the quickest, typically reaching cooking temperature in 10-15 minutes. Pizza ovens take longer but reach significantly higher temperatures. Griddles with electric or gas heating also heat relatively quickly.

What's the most cost-effective option for a beginner?

Gas grills are typically the most affordable entry point for outdoor cooking, offering excellent versatility and ease of use. However, Big Horn Outdoors offers quality options at various price points across all cooker types.

Can I use a griddle for grilling?

While griddles and grills serve different purposes, some modern outdoor cooking stations combine both surfaces. A griddle excels at flat-top cooking while grills are better for direct flame searing.

How much space do I need for a pizza oven?

Most pizza ovens require at least 4x4 feet of dedicated space, plus surrounding clearance for safety and entertaining. Big Horn Outdoors offers various sizes to fit different yard configurations.
