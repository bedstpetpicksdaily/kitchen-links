Frequently Asked Questions

Is an 8 quart Instant Pot big enough for a family of 6?

Yes, an 8 quart Instant Pot is ideal for a family of 6, allowing you to cook meals for everyone in one batch. The usable capacity is typically around 6 quarts (you fill it 2/3 full for safety), which comfortably serves 4-6 people depending on portion sizes and whether you're cooking grains, meat, or stews.

What's the difference between 6 quart and 8 quart Instant Pots?

The 8 quart model has 33% more capacity than the 6 quart, letting you cook for larger families or meal prep in bigger batches. The 8 quart takes slightly longer to reach pressure but cooks food at the same speed once pressurized, and requires more counter and storage space.

Will an 8 quart Instant Pot fit on my kitchen counter?

An 8 quart Instant Pot is approximately 16 inches wide, 8 inches deep, and 9 inches tall, so you'll need dedicated counter space or storage. Measure your available space before purchasing, as it's significantly larger than standard kitchen appliances and may not fit under cabinets.

Can you use an 8 quart Instant Pot for small meals or just big batches?

You can use an 8 quart Instant Pot for small meals, but it's less efficient since you need to fill it at least 1/3 full for proper pressure cooking. For flexibility, consider if you'll primarily cook large batches or meal prep, as a smaller model might be more practical if you frequently cook for 2-3 people.
