Frequently Asked Questions

How many cups does the Chemex Classic make?

The Chemex comes in multiple sizes, with the most popular 6-cup model brewing approximately 30 ounces. It also offers 3-cup and 10-cup versions to suit different household needs.

What type of filters does the Chemex use?

Chemex uses proprietary paper filters that are 20-30% thicker than standard coffee filters, removing more oils and sediment for a cleaner cup of coffee.

Is the Chemex difficult to clean?

No, the Chemex is extremely easy to clean. Simply discard used filters and grounds, rinse the glass vessel with warm water, and occasionally hand wash with mild soap for deeper cleaning.

Can I use regular coffee filters in a Chemex?

While you technically can, Chemex-branded filters are recommended. Their superior thickness and fit optimize the brewing process and coffee quality that the Chemex is designed to deliver.
