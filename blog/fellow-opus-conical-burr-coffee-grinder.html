Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Fellow Opus better than blade grinders?

Yes, significantly. Conical burr grinders like the Opus produce uniform particle size, while blade grinders create inconsistent grounds that negatively impact extraction and taste. Burr grinders are essential for specialty coffee preparation.

How many cups of coffee can the Opus grind at once?

The Fellow Opus has a hopper capacity of approximately 1/2 pound of beans, sufficient for grinding 8-10 cups of coffee depending on your grind size setting.

Is the Fellow Opus loud?

While electric burr grinders are inherently louder than manual options, the Opus operates at a reasonable noise level for its class, typically around 70-80 decibels during operation.

Can I use the Fellow Opus for espresso?

Absolutely. The Opus includes fine grind settings specifically calibrated for espresso machines, making it suitable for espresso, Turkish coffee, and ultra-fine grinds.
