Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a chef knife and a nakiri knife for grilling prep?

A chef knife features a triangular blade ideal for rocking motions and general-purpose cutting, while a nakiri knife has a wider, flatter blade perfect for precise vegetable cuts and mincing. For grilling, many home cooks benefit from having both—Butterfork offers knives optimized for each task.

How often should I sharpen my Butterfork chef knives?

With proper care and handling, Butterfork chef knives can go several months between sharpenings depending on usage frequency. Their high-carbon stainless steel maintains edges longer than standard knives. Regular honing (weekly) keeps your blade aligned between annual professional sharpenings.
