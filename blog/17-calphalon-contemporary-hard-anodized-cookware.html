Frequently Asked Questions

Is Calphalon Contemporary cookware oven-safe?

Yes, most pieces in the Calphalon Contemporary line are oven-safe up to 400°F, making them versatile for stovetop-to-oven cooking.

Does Calphalon Contemporary cookware work on induction stoves?

No, this line is not induction-compatible due to its aluminum construction. You'll need cookware with magnetic bottoms for induction cooking.

How long does the non-stick coating last?

With proper care and hand-washing, the non-stick coating typically lasts 3-5 years of regular use before showing signs of wear.

Can I use metal utensils with Calphalon Contemporary cookware?

It's recommended to use silicone, wood, or nylon utensils to preserve the non-stick coating. Metal utensils can scratch and damage the surface.

Is Calphalon Contemporary cookware dishwasher-safe?

While technically dishwasher-safe, hand-washing is recommended to extend the life of the non-stick coating and prevent premature wear.
