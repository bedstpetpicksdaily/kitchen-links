The Environmental Impact: Plastic Wrap vs. Bee's Wrap

Traditional plastic wrap is a petroleum-based product designed for single use. Once you're done with it, it typically ends up in the trash, destined for a landfill where it persists for 400+ years without breaking down. Beyond landfills, microplastics from degraded wrap contaminate soil and water systems, harming wildlife and entering our food chain. During summer months when food storage needs skyrocket, plastic consumption intensifies dramatically.

Bee's Wrap offers a refreshing alternative. Made from organic cotton infused with sustainably sourced beeswax, plant resin, and jojoba oil, these wraps are 100% natural, compostable, and biodegradable. Each wrap lasts up to one year with proper care, replacing hundreds of single-use plastic sheets. At the end of its life, Bee's Wrap breaks down harmlessly in your compost bin within five months. This circular approach to food storage eliminates waste while supporting regenerative agriculture practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Bee's Wrap in the microwave or with hot foods?

No, Bee's Wrap is not microwave-safe and should not be used with hot foods above 85°F, as high heat can damage the beeswax coating. For warm or hot foods, use alternative glass containers or ceramic covers. Bee's Wrap excels with cold and room-temperature items.

How long does one Bee's Wrap last?

With proper care—hand washing with cool water and mild soap, air drying, and proper storage away from heat—a Bee's Wrap lasts approximately one year. After that, you can refresh it or compost it naturally.

Is Bee's Wrap safe for food contact?

Yes, absolutely. Bee's Wrap is made from organic cotton, sustainably sourced beeswax, plant resin, and jojoba oil—all food-safe, natural materials. There are no toxic chemicals, BPA, or plastic coatings that could leach into your food.

How do I clean and maintain my Bee's Wrap?

Hand wash with cool water and mild soap immediately after use, then air dry on a clean surface. Avoid hot water and direct heat. Store flat or loosely rolled in a cool, dry place. With proper care, your wraps will remain effective for a full year.

Does Bee's Wrap work as well as plastic wrap for storage?

For cold and room-temperature foods, Bee's Wrap actually outperforms plastic wrap due to its moldable, flexible nature and superior seal. It's ideal for bowls, produce, sandwiches, and bread. For hot foods, you'll need alternative solutions since Bee's Wrap isn't heat-resistant.
