Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Butterfork chef knives and nakiri knives?

Chef knives feature a curved blade ideal for rocking and chopping, while nakiri knives have straight edges and wider blades perfect for precision vegetable slicing and mincing.

Do Butterfork knife sets include a knife stand?

Many Butterfork sets include attractive knife stands, though some starter sets may not. Check individual product descriptions to confirm what's included with your chosen set.

How should I maintain my Butterfork kitchen knives?

Hand wash immediately after use with warm soapy water and dry thoroughly. Use a honing steel regularly and sharpen annually. Avoid dishwashers and cutting boards that dull blades quickly.

Which Butterfork knife set is best for beginners?

The Butterfork Essential Starter Set is perfect for beginners, providing three essential knives—chef, paring, and bread knife—without overwhelming investment.

Can I buy individual Butterfork knives or must I purchase complete sets?

Butterfork offers both individual knife options and complete sets, allowing you to customize your collection based on your specific needs and budget.
