  • True Convection Technology: Look for air fryers that explicitly feature convection heating with multiple heating elements and a powerful fan system.   • Capacity That Matches Your Cooking Style: Choose between 4-6 quart models for individuals or small families, or jump to 8+ quarts if you're cooking for groups or meal-prepping.
