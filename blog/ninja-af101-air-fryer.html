What size meals can the Ninja AF101 prepare?

The AF101's compact basket holds approximately 4.2 quarts, making it suitable for 1-2 people or small side dishes for a family. It works best for individual portions and smaller meals rather than large family dinners.

How long does the Ninja AF101 take to preheat?

The Ninja AF101 preheats in approximately 2-3 minutes, allowing you to start cooking quickly. This fast preheat time is one of the standout features for busy home cooks.

Is the Ninja AF101 easy to clean?

Yes, the AF101 features a non-stick basket and crisper plate that are dishwasher-safe, making cleanup simple. The compact design also means less surface area to maintain compared to larger air fryers.

What foods cook best in the Ninja AF101?

The AF101 excels at cooking frozen foods, chicken wings, vegetables, and French fries. Its consistent heating produces crispy exteriors while keeping interiors moist, making it versatile for various recipes.
