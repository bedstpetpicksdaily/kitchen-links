Frequently Asked Questions

What's the wattage of the Ninja Professional Blender?

The Ninja Professional Blender typically features a 1000+ watt motor, providing sufficient power for most blending tasks including crushing ice, frozen fruits, and tough vegetables.

Is the Ninja Professional Blender dishwasher safe?

How loud is the Ninja Professional Blender?

Operating at around 80-85 decibels, it's comparable to other high-powered blenders. It's louder than immersion blenders but typical for full-size blenders.

Can the Ninja Professional Blender make hot soup?

Yes, friction from the powerful motor generates heat, allowing you to blend ingredients into hot soup without additional cooking.
