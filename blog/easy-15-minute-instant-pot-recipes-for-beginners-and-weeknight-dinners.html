What to Avoid

  • Don't skip the liquid – Instant Pots need minimum liquid to create steam and build pressure (usually at least 1 cup). Beginners sometimes try to recreate stovetop cooking by reducing liquids, which can result in a "burn" warning and failed meals. For 15-minute recipes, always follow the liquid measurements exactly.
  • Avoid overcrowding the pot or stacking ingredients – When you pile frozen chicken breasts on top of each other or cram in too many vegetables, they cook unevenly and the bottom layer may burn. For quick meals, spread ingredients in a single layer or use a trivet to separate levels.
