Frequently Asked Questions

What types of coffee can the Nespresso VertuoPlus make?

The VertuoPlus can brew espresso, double espresso, gran lungo, coffee, and alto coffee using Nespresso's compatible Vertuo capsules. Each size is automatically detected and adjusted for optimal brewing.

How long does it take to brew a cup?

The machine heats up in approximately 15-20 seconds and brews most drinks in under one minute, making it ideal for quick morning preparation.

Is the VertuoPlus easy to clean and maintain?

Yes, it features automatic capsule ejection and a removable water tank. The machine has a self-cleaning function and requires minimal manual maintenance between uses.

Can I use regular coffee pods with the VertuoPlus?

No, the VertuoPlus is designed exclusively for Nespresso Vertuo capsules. Standard K-Cup or other third-party pods are not compatible with this model.

What is the water tank capacity?

The VertuoPlus comes with a 40-ounce water tank, providing enough capacity for multiple cups of coffee before requiring a refill.
