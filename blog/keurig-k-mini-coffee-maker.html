Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Keurig K-Mini take to brew?

The K-Mini brews a full cup of coffee in approximately 2-3 minutes, depending on your selected cup size and water temperature.

What cup sizes can the K-Mini brew?

The machine offers three brewing options: 6 oz, 8 oz, and 10 oz cups, perfect for customizing your coffee strength and portion size.

Is the Keurig K-Mini easy to clean and maintain?

Yes, the K-Mini has a removable drip tray and reservoir for easy cleaning. Descaling is recommended every 3-6 months to keep the machine running smoothly.

Can you use reusable K-Cup filters with the K-Mini?

Yes, the K-Mini is compatible with reusable K-Cup filters, allowing you to brew your own ground coffee and reduce waste.

How much counter space does the K-Mini require?

The K-Mini measures just 12.1 x 4.3 x 7.2 inches, making it one of the smallest Keurig models and ideal for compact kitchens.
