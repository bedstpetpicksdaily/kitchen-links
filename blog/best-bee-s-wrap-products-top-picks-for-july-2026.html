Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Bee's Wrap products last?

With proper care and hand washing, Bee's Wrap products typically last for approximately one year of regular use. The lifespan depends on frequency of use and proper maintenance, such as hand washing in cool water and allowing them to air dry completely.

Can you use Bee's Wrap in the microwave or dishwasher?

Bee's Wrap should not be used in the microwave as heat will damage the beeswax coating. While some bowl covers are labeled as dishwasher-safe, hand washing in cool water is recommended to extend the product's lifespan and maintain the beeswax integrity.

Are Bee's Wrap products suitable for all food types?

Bee's Wrap works best with room-temperature or cold foods. Avoid using them to wrap hot foods, as the beeswax will melt. They're perfect for cheese, bread, fresh produce, sandwiches, and covering bowls of prepared meals after they've cooled.

What makes Bee's Wrap different from other beeswax wrap brands?

Bee's Wrap pioneered the beeswax wrap category and maintains strict standards for organic cotton, sustainably harvested beeswax, and plant-based resins. Their commitment to transparency and eco-friendly production methods, combined with innovative product designs like bowl covers and bread bags, sets them apart in the market.
