Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Bee's Wrap beeswax wraps last?

With proper care and hand washing, Bee's Wrap products typically last up to one year of regular use. The beeswax naturally wears over time, but many users find them effective for much longer depending on usage frequency.

Can Bee's Wrap wraps be used in the refrigerator or freezer?

Bee's Wrap wraps work best in cool to room-temperature conditions. While they can be used in the refrigerator, they're not recommended for freezer storage as the beeswax can become brittle in extreme cold.

Are Bee's Wrap products completely plastic-free?

Yes, Bee's Wrap products are made from organic cotton, sustainable beeswax, and tree resin. They're entirely plastic-free, both in the product itself and in their eco-friendly, plastic-free packaging.

What's the difference between Bee's Wrap bowl covers and bread storage bags?

Bowl covers are designed to stretch over various bowl sizes, creating an airtight seal for salads, dips, and prepared dishes. Bread storage bags are specially shaped bags ideal for artisan bread and baked goods, allowing them to breathe while staying fresh.

Can I use Bee's Wrap wraps in the microwave or oven?

No, Bee's Wrap should never be used in microwaves or ovens as heat can melt the beeswax coating. They're designed for food storage and wrapping, not heating.
