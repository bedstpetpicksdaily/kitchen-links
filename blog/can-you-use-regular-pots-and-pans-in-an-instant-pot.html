The Short Answer

Regular pots and pans are not designed to fit properly inside an Instant Pot's cooking chamber or to withstand its pressurized environment. The Instant Pot requires its specific stainless steel inner pot to function safely and effectively. While you can use certain oven-safe cookware as accessories within the pot using a trivet or steamer rack, they serve different purposes than direct cooking vessels. Always consult your Instant Pot manual before using any cookware inside the appliance.

The Full Explanation

Understanding why regular cookware doesn't work in an Instant Pot comes down to design and safety. The Instant Pot's inner pot is engineered specifically for pressure cooking—it has precise measurements, proper thickness, and specific material composition that allows it to seal correctly and distribute heat evenly under pressure. Regular pots and pans lack these specifications.

Sizing Issues: Standard kitchen pots typically won't fit inside an Instant Pot's chamber. The Instant Pot comes in various sizes (3-quart, 6-quart, 8-quart), and only cookware that fits within these dimensions without touching the heating element or lid can be used as accessories.

Pressure Seal Problems: The Instant Pot's inner pot has a specific shape and design that works with the appliance's sealing ring to maintain proper pressure. Using a different pot compromises the seal, which can lead to pressure loss, incomplete cooking, or dangerous pressure release.

Heat Distribution: The inner pot's thickness and material composition ensure even heat distribution during high-pressure cooking. Regular cookware may have thin spots that cause uneven cooking or burning, particularly since the Instant Pot uses direct heat on the bottom of the vessel.

What You CAN Use: While the inner pot itself must remain the original, you can use certain oven-safe glass bowls, silicone molds, or smaller pots placed on a trivet inside the Instant Pot for steaming or water bath cooking. These items don't replace the inner pot but rather sit inside it for specific cooking methods.
