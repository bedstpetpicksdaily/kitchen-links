  • Independent Basket Controls: The best dual basket air fryers let you set different temperatures and cook times for each basket. Models with separate timers give you complete flexibility for truly efficient batch cooking.
  • Minimum 8-Quart Total Capacity: For meaningful meal prep, you want baskets that are 4 quarts each or larger. Adequate space means fewer repeat cooking cycles and more time saved overall.
  • Even Heat Distribution and Quick Preheat: Dual basket models can have hot spots since air needs to circulate between two cooking chambers. Look for air fryers with perforated dividers or staggered basket designs that promote consistent heat.
