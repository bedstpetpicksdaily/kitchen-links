Frequently Asked Questions

What types of drinks can the Nespresso Vertuo Pop make?

The Vertuo Pop can brew espresso, double espresso, gran lungo, coffee, and large coffee using compatible Vertuo capsules. You can also make hot water for tea and other beverages.

How much counter space does the Vertuo Pop require?

The Vertuo Pop has dimensions of approximately 4.3 x 12.8 inches, making it one of the most compact Nespresso models available and ideal for small kitchens.

Are Nespresso Vertuo capsules recyclable?

Yes, Nespresso capsules are made from aluminum and are recyclable. Nespresso offers free capsule recycling programs and provides recycling bags to customers.

What's the warranty on the Vertuo Pop?

Nespresso typically offers a one-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options may be available through retailers.

How long does it take to brew a cup of coffee?

The Vertuo Pop brews most coffee drinks in under 60 seconds from the moment you insert the capsule and press the button.
