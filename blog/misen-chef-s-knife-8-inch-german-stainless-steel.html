What makes German stainless steel better for chef's knives?

German stainless steel offers superior edge retention, corrosion resistance, and durability compared to standard kitchen knives. It maintains sharpness longer and requires less frequent sharpening, making it ideal for regular kitchen use.

Yes, an 8-inch blade is considered the professional standard and works perfectly for most home cooking tasks including slicing meats, chopping vegetables, and mincing herbs with precision and efficiency.

How do I maintain the Misen Chef's Knife's sharpness?
