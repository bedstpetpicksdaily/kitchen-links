How does this compare to other Damascus chef knives at similar price points?

Is hand-washing really necessary, or am I being paranoid about Damascus care?

You're not paranoid—you're being realistic. The folded steel layers in Damascus are gorgeous, but they're also slightly porous compared to solid stainless. Dishwashers create prolonged moisture exposure and temperature fluctuations that encourage corrosion in those micro-gaps. During July's humidity especially, hand-wash and dry immediately. It takes 90 seconds, and it protects a significant investment. If you're not willing to do this, acknowledge it now and pick a different knife.
