Frequently Asked Questions

Does the OXO garlic press work with unpeeled garlic?

While it can technically press unpeeled garlic, OXO recommends peeling cloves first. The innovative built-in chamber helps remove skins easily, making the extra step quick and convenient.

Is the OXO Good Grips Garlic Press dishwasher safe?

Yes, it's completely dishwasher safe. You can throw both the handles and pressing chamber in the top rack for easy cleanup without worrying about hand-washing residue.

How much garlic does it press at once?

The press accommodates one standard garlic clove per use, producing about one teaspoon of minced garlic per clove, depending on size and density.

Can it press other ingredients besides garlic?

Absolutely. Many users successfully press ginger, nuts, and herbs. However, very hard items may strain the mechanism, so stick to softer produce for best results.
