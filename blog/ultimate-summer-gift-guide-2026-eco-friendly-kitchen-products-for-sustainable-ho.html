Bee's Wrap products typically last up to one year with proper care. The lifespan depends on frequency of use and washing methods. Most users find they significantly outlast their investment compared to the cost of replacing plastic wrap continuously.

Can Bee's Wrap be used in the microwave or oven?

No, Bee's Wrap should not be used in microwaves or ovens as the beeswax will melt. These wraps are best for room-temperature and refrigerated food storage. For hot foods, use glass lids or other microwave-safe alternatives.
