Frequently Asked Questions

How long do Bee's Wrap products actually last?

With proper care and hand washing, Bee's Wrap products typically last 1-2 years before requiring replacement. Most users find they outlast hundreds of rolls of plastic wrap, making them economically superior despite higher initial cost.

Can Bee's Wrap be used in the freezer?

Bee's Wrap works best at room temperature and warm conditions where the beeswax becomes pliable. While brief freezer storage is possible, extended freezing can crack the coating. For frozen items, consider glass containers as a complementary storage solution.

How should I care for my Bee's Wrap to maximize lifespan?

Hand wash with cool water and mild soap immediately after use. Air dry completely before storage. Avoid hot water and dishwashers, which can damage the beeswax coating. Store flat or loosely rolled in a cool, dry location away from direct heat.

Are Bee's Wrap products suitable for raw meat storage?

Bee's Wrap is not recommended for raw meat storage due to potential cross-contamination. Use them for cheeses, vegetables, fruits, baked goods, and prepared foods. For raw meat, rely on glass containers or dedicated storage solutions.
