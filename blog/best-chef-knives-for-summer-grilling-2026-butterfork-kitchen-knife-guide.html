What size chef knife is best for grilling prep?

An 8-inch chef knife is ideal for most grilling tasks. Butterfork's 8-inch chef knife balances control and versatility, handling everything from vegetable slicing to meat trimming without being unwieldy for detailed work.

Are Butterfork knives suitable for outdoor use?

Yes. Butterfork uses high-carbon stainless steel construction that resists corrosion and moisture exposure. Their knives maintain performance in outdoor conditions when properly cleaned and stored, making them excellent for grilling environments.

What's the difference between a chef knife and a nakiri knife?

Chef knives feature a curved blade for rocking cuts, while nakiri knives have a flat blade optimized for push-cutting. Butterfork's nakiri excels at vegetable preparation, while their chef knives offer greater versatility across proteins and produce.

Do Butterfork knife sets include storage solutions?

Many Butterfork knife sets include knife stands or block storage. A dedicated stand keeps your knives organized, accessible, and protected while grilling, and keeps your outdoor prep area safer.

How often should I sharpen Butterfork knives?

Butterfork's high-quality blades maintain sharpness longer than budget alternatives, typically requiring sharpening 2-3 times per year with regular home use. Proper hand-washing and storage extend intervals between sharpenings.
