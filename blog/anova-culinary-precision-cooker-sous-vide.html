Frequently Asked Questions

How long does sous vide cooking take?

Sous vide cooking times vary by food type and thickness, typically ranging from 30 minutes to several hours. The Anova maintains precise temperatures throughout, ensuring perfect results regardless of cooking duration.

Do I need special bags for sous vide cooking?

You can use vacuum-sealed bags, freezer bags, or reusable silicone bags. For best results, remove as much air as possible before cooking to ensure proper heat transfer.

Is the Anova Precision Cooker easy to clean?

Yes, the device is simple to clean with just soap and water. Remove it from the pot and wipe down the exterior. Never submerge the heating element.

Can the Anova control multiple devices?

The app allows you to control one Anova device at a time, though you can manage multiple devices sequentially through the same account.
